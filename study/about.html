Study Bunny is a learning assistant that helps you stay on top of your tasks!

Need to keep track of your assessments? This app contains a calendar that you can post due dates and assessments to work on. The calendar gives options on what type of assessments to focus on at home.

It comes with its own calculator to make quick calculations.

We provided a section to take notes from school lessons and a drop-down box to choose what class the notes are from.

With Study Bunny, you will never fall behind in your assessments.
