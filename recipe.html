Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 1h30m
  • Serves: 3-4 trays of cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
White sugar 3/4 cups
Brown sugar 3/4 cups
cocoa powder 4 tbps
Butter 1 cups
Chocolate chips 1 bag

Step 1: Mix together dry ingredients.

Step 2: Mix together wet ingredients.

Step 3: In three sections, mix in the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.

Step 4: preheat oven to 350.

Step 5: Make cookie shapes on a tray with parchment paper on it.

Step 6: Bake for 9-12 min or until golden brown.

Enjoy!



Salad

  • Time: 20min
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 1/2 a head
grape tomatoes many
Mixed Seeds 1/3 cups
salad dressing 1to taste
Cucumber 1

Step 1: Cut up lettuce an cucumbers. (be careful!)

Step 2: Add lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes and seeds into a large bowl.

Step 3: Add salad dressing and serve.

Enjoy!



Sandwich

  • Time: 5min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 1
bread slice 2
Salami 1
Cheese slice 1

Step 1: Cut up a piece of lettuce.

Step 2: Cook salami on a pan

Step 3: Place a slice of bread, then a piece of lettuce, then the salami and finally the piece of cheese.
